About ColorFlipper

ColorFlipper is a clean and interactive web experience designed to let users instantly transform the background with a single click. With predefined color options and a dynamic random generator, it offers a fast and engaging way to explore visual variations.

The interface focuses on simplicity, clarity, and responsiveness. Every interaction delivers immediate feedback, creating a smooth and satisfying user experience across devices.
